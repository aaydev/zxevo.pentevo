Changes in ZX-Evolution Rev.CS2:

1. Added HDAUDIO connector.
2. Added MICRO-SD slot.
3. X9 is KEMPSTON SEGA GAMEPAD connector, and second redefine GAMEPAD, not RS232 now.
4. X19 is RS232 TTL-standard.
5. BAT54C replaced by SS14.
6. ATX connector is universal for 4.2/5.5 pins step.
7. Added EXT_VGA connector for External video source, like VDAC2 or etc.
8. Replaced FT232 to CP2102.
